Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

Thank you for using our SmartCalc Pro App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics to improve app performance and understand how users interact with the app.
  • Crash Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, which help us diagnose and fix issues.
  • Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) is used to send general-purpose notifications. No personally identifiable information is collected through this service.

2. Internet Usage

  • Most calculator features (e.g., BMI, Area, Mass, etc.) work fully offline.
  • The Currency Converter feature may access the internet to retrieve live exchange rates, but it does not collect personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Fix bugs and enhance stability
  • Send general notifications (e.g., update reminders)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. All analytics and crash data is processed by Google Firebase in compliance with its privacy policies.

5. Firebase Services

Our app uses Google Firebase services such as Analytics, Crashlytics, and Cloud Messaging. These tools help us improve the app experience, monitor errors, and send general notifications.

Firebase may collect certain device or usage data as part of its service. All data is processed according to Firebase’s Privacy Policy.

We encourage users to read the Firebase Privacy Policy to better understand how data is handled: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Advertising

This app does not display ads and does not use any advertising SDKs.

7. User Control and Consent

You may disable analytics tracking by adjusting your device settings (e.g., limit ad tracking or deny usage access).

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
